Today is a really sad day for me. My beloved Podcast-Client [Instacast](http://vemedio.com/products/instacast) has closed down due to „no money left“. I normally can remember very vividly things or places that that I’ve been to when listening to spoken words. Therefore the following list is something personal.

There is basically **two things** in Instacast [that kept me from switching for all those years](https://twitter.com/toadle/status/596247463830417408):

1. The Mac-desktop client that synced flawlessly with the iOS-apps
2. The **list of favorites** that I used heavily to collect all the best episodes that I came across.

Since I’m now in danger of losing these favorites, I thought I could compile this list of episodes here for your listening/reading-pleasure.

To my shock this dates back up until 2010, where I added my first episode. Some of these are german. Sorry, but that is the way it is ;-)!

RIP Instacast, you’ll be missed.

## 2010

- [CRE151 Die ARM-Architektur](http://cre.fm/cre151-die-arm-architektur)
Great german talk about the ARM-processor-architecture by [Chaos Radio-Express](http://cre.fm/). I remember listening to this while driving through the Egyptian desert in a tourist bus, back from the „Valley of kings“. 

- [CRE 163 Ruby On Rails](http://cre.fm/cre163-ruby-und-rails)
also by [Chaos Radio-Express](http://cre.fm/)

## 2011

- [Das atomare Vermächtnis der Nordmeerflotte](http://www.ard.de/home/radio/Das_atomare_Vermaechtnis_der_Nordmeerflotte/517030/index.html)
a german feature about the Russian North sea fleet which’s radioactive remains rott away on the Kola island.

## 2012

- [CRE 195 Das Gehirn](http://cre.fm/cre195-das-gehirn?utm_source=feedburner&utm_medium=feed&utm_campaign=Feed%3A+cre-podcast+%28CRE+%28M4A%29%29)
a thorough description of the inner workings of the brain. I love how [Chaos Radio-Express](http://cre.fm/) goes into the details.

- [Anna Kendrick](http://nerdist.libsyn.com/anna-kendrick) 
This episode by the [Nerdist Podcast](http://nerdist.com/podcasts/nerdist-podcast-channel/) changed Anna Kendrick’s image for me. Really funny woman with a strong opinion.

- [angespielt #042 - XCOM: Enemy Unknown](http://blog.richter.fm/podcast/angespielt/20121012/angespielt-042-xcom-enemy-unknown?utm_source=feedburner&utm_medium=feed&utm_campaign=Feed%3A+MonoxydAngespielt+%28monoxyd+%C2%BB+angespielt%29)

- [CR184 Technik und Krieg](http://chaosradio.ccc.de/cr184.html)
Interesting topic and episode.

## 2013

- [Sasha Grey](http://nerdist.libsyn.com/sasha-grey)
Nuff said.

-  [Chris Hadfield](http://nerdist.libsyn.com/chris-hadfield)

## 2015

- [Gillian Anderson](http://nerdist.libsyn.com/gillian-anderson) Scully talks about how she accidentally became part of a hit-show. 

- [17: Final Exit](http://thisiscriminal.libsyn.com/episode-17-final-exit?utm_source=feedburner&utm_medium=feed&utm_campaign=Feed%3A+CriminalShow+%28Criminal%29) The [Criminal](http://thisiscriminal.libsyn.com/) podcast is the next best thing since Serial. This episode tells a story about an organization that helps people commit suicide. Crazy stuff. 

- [Absturz über den Alpen - Erste Ermittlungsergebnisse zur Flugzeugkatastrophe](http://ondemand-mp3.dradio.de/file/dradio/2015/03/26/dlf_20150326_1840_c955c96f.mp3) One of the few reports about the [Germanwings crash](http://toadle.me/2015/03/29/die-katastrophe-und-die-berichterstattung.html) that one could actually bear to listen to

- [Episode 3 - Jay’s Day](https://audioboom.com/boos/3175195-episode-3-jay-s-day) Since the [Serial](http://serialpodcast.org/) podcast concluded without a real opinion (the only way to go IMHO). This is a podcast by the team that tries to free the convicted murder. This episode reveals a twist about the investigating police-team.

- [Die große Erziehungssendung](http://media.rbb-online.de/rad/podcast/zwei_alte_hasen/zh_20150531_175700.mp3) the german podcast [Sanft & Sorgfältig](http://www.radioeins.de/archiv/podcast/zwei_alte_hasen.html) is not only funny, but can also teach you some things. Which it does here. 

Hope this list helps you find something nice to listen to.
